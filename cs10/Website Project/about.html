About

Elder Scrolls Online is the most recent addition to the Elder Scrolls universe.

The Elder Scrolls series has been around since 1994, with the release of The Elder Scrolls: Arena by Bethesda Softworks. Since then, 4 main additions, and countless spinoffs have been released, with the most popular being The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim, the most recent main addition to the series.

List of Playable Races

In ESO you can play as 10 different races: High Elf (Altmer), Wood Elf (Bosmer), Dark Elf (Dunmer), Argonian (Saxhleel), Khajiit, Redguard, Breton, Orc (Orsimer), Nord and Imperial (only available to players with the Imperial Edition Upgrade).

Each race has it's own "racial passives", passive abilities that give different abilities.

List of Playable Classes

In ESO you can play as 6 different classes: Dragonknight, Necromancer, Nightblade, Sorcerer, Templar and Warden.

Each class has 3 full skill lines, each with 6 active abilities and 4 passive abilities.
